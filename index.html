Do you offer free collection and delivery?

Yes. Free doorstep collection and delivery is included across Durban and surrounding areas. We collect your items, care for them, and return them fresh and on time.

How do I book a collection?

Message us on WhatsApp or call 082 267 9862. We'll confirm your items, your location and a collection time that suits your schedule.

Do you clean carpets and blankets?

Absolutely. We deep-clean carpets and rugs, restore patterned and faded carpets, and professionally wash and sanitise blankets for a fresh, hygienic finish.

Can you treat delicate or special garments?

Yes. Delicate and special fabrics receive careful, tailored treatment — gentle washing, pressing and protection so your favourite pieces last longer.

Do you rent chairs and tables for events?

We do. Our chairs and tables are clean, sturdy and event-ready for weddings, functions, parties and community gatherings — delivered and set up for you.

How much does it cost?

Carpet cleaning starts from R90 and blanket cleaning from R100, with free collection and delivery included. Laundry, garment care, carpet restoration and rentals are quoted on request based on volume — message us for a fast, fair quote.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve Durban and surrounding areas, including Westville, Pinetown and Durban North. Not sure if we cover you? Send us a message and we'll confirm.
